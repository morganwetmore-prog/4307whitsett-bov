A Realistic Price We Can Deliver Beats an Inflated Price That Sits

Christine, when you evaluate BOVs, the number that matters is not the list price on page one — it's the price you actually close at, and how quickly. Our 96% sale-to-list ratio across 459 closed transactions means we price correctly and back it up. A listing that opens at $6.7M and trades at $6.4M — after 90+ days of negotiations and buyer fallouts — produces a worse outcome than a $6.5M listing that closes in 36 days. Our track record is the evidence. We don't chase listings with numbers we can't support.
